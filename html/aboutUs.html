Our Story

2019

WheelHub was founded with a vision to redefine the automotive experience. Our journey began with a small team of passionate individuals who believed in the power of quality, reliability, and customer-centric service. From the outset, we were committed to offering a range of vehicles that catered to diverse needs and preferences, setting the stage for a new era in automotive sales.

2021

2021 was a year of significant growth and expansion. We introduced new services, including comprehensive maintenance packages and flexible financing options, making car ownership more accessible and convenient for our customers. Our commitment to sustainability also led us to expand our range of hybrid and electric vehicles, aligning with our goal of promoting environmentally responsible driving.

2023

In 2023, we focused on giving back to the community that has supported us throughout our journey. We initiated various community outreach programs, supporting local charities and environmental causes. Our commitment to corporate social responsibility became a core aspect of our operations, reflecting our belief in making a positive impact beyond the automotive industry.
